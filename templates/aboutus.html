Our Story

SiloganExpress began as a humble eatery in the heart of Cebu, born from a passion for serving hearty, affordable Filipino comfort food. Since our first branch opened in Lower Calajoan, Minglanilla, we’ve been dedicated to bringing the authentic taste of silog meals to every table. Our journey is fueled by love for food and community — making every bite a taste of home.

Our Mission

To serve delicious, high-quality silog meals that bring joy and satisfaction to every customer, using fresh ingredients and heartfelt service.

Our Vision

To be the go-to destination for Filipino comfort food, spreading the love for silog across the Philippines and beyond.
